How to book Student fares

STEP-1

Register for Travel ID

To book a student fare, you need to have a valid Travel ID. You can register for Travel ID online or by calling the Travel ID hotline.

STEP-2

Verify Student

Once you are registered and logged in to your Travel ID, you will be able to verify your student status in the 'Profile' section of your account.You only need to do this once.

STEP-3

Search and book student fares

Always search and book student fares from this page. You must be logged in with a verified student status in order to proceed.When selecting your flights, student fares can be clearly identified with a "Student" icon.
